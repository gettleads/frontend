Frequently Asked Questions

What is a credit?

One credit lets you unlock a single contact's details — either their email, phone, or both depending on your plan.

Do unused credits roll over?

Credits reset every month and do not roll over. Enterprise plans can negotiate custom terms.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees. You keep access until the end of your billing period.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes — the Pro plan comes with a 7-day free trial and 100 credits. No credit card required to start.

How accurate is the data?

We achieve 97%+ email accuracy through real-time validation. Phone numbers are verified against carrier databases.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Enterprise plans are fully custom-priced. Contact our sales team for a quote tailored to your team's size and needs.
